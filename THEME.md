# PlantGlow Theme — 2026-05-10

## Brand Essence
**PlantGlow** — All-in-one plant growing database for urban people.

## Core Problem
Urban planting is a good habit, but plants often don't grow well. People waste time searching everywhere for care information.

## Solution
PlantGlow is an all-in-one plant growing database that helps urban people save time searching everywhere, so they can spend more time enjoying indoor plant growing.

## Tagline / Hero Messaging
- **Headline:** Indoor Growing, Simplified
- **Sub-headline:** All-in-one plant database with care guides, space matching, and growing tips — so you spend less time researching and more time enjoying your plants.

## Positioning
- For urban people who want to grow plants but don't want to spend hours researching
- One destination for everything plant-related
- Save time, enjoy growing

## Content Pillars
1. Plant Database — 20+ plants with detailed care guides
2. Space Checker — match plants to your room's conditions
3. Care Guides — clear, actionable tips for every plant
4. Grow with Confidence — stop guessing, start thriving

## Brand Voice
- Confident but approachable
- Practical, not preachy
- Saves time, enables enjoyment
- Urban-focused

## Color Palette
- Forest greens: #22c55e, #16a34a, #15803d, #166534, #14532d
- Lime accent: #a3e635, #84cc16
- Background: white + #f0fdf4 (forest-50)

## Typography
- Font: Plus Jakarta Sans
- Feel: Modern, clean, trustworthy

## Social Media Positioning (Instagram, etc.)
- Content: Plant tips, care guides, before/after transformations
- CTA: "Visit PlantGlow to find your perfect plant"
- Tone: Visual, shareable, educational
